Short Bio

I am currently a Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I previously worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Computer Science at the University of British Columbia (UBC) and a Research Fellow in the School of Computing at the National University of Singapore (NUS). I received my Ph.D. from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), under the supervision of Prof. Xiaokui Xiao and Prof. Aixin Sun. Before that, I got my B.Eng. from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I am a long-term visiting scholar of the AI Group of Prof. Pietro Liò at the University of Cambridge.

Research Interests

My research is centered on data management & analysis, and machine learning. My work has focused on developing scalable, efficient, and theoretically grounded algorithms for graph analytics and graph learning. In graph learning, I am particularly interested in scalable and effective Graph Neural Networks. In parallel, I also study cost-efficient and performance-guaranteed strategies for Large Language Model ensembles.
